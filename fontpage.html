I strove with none,
For none was worth my strife.
Nature I loved and,
Next to Nature, Art;
I warm'd both hands before the fire of life;
It sinks,
And I am ready to depart.

我和谁都不争，
和谁争我都不屑；
我爱大自然，
其次就是艺术；
我双手烤着生命之火取暖；
火萎了，
我也准备走了。
     ---兰德《生与死》
